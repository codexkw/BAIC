X55 II

Vogue Beast Arrival
Test Drive View VR

Vogue Beast Design

The X55 II carries BAIC's Capital Beauty design language into the SUV segment. Bold, dynamic lines with a powerful stance create a strong visual presence on the road.

Refined Interior

Dual 10-inch screens — LCD instrument cluster and central infotainment display. Apple CarPlay and EasyConnection mobile connectivity. Dual-zone automatic climate control with PM2.5 air purification and integrated refrigerator in the central armrest area.

Digital Cockpit

10-inch LCD instrument screen paired with 10-inch central control display. Multi-color interior ambient lighting creates an immersive driving atmosphere. Voice control and wireless charging available on Luxury trim.

Hidden Door Handle

Mechanical on Honor/Premium, inductive on Luxury trim

18"/19" Alloy Wheels

225/60 R18 two-tone on Honor/Premium, 225/55 R19 gray on Luxury

Comprehensive Safety

ESP, ABS+EBD, EBA, HAC, HDC, and BOS brake override system. Driver and passenger airbags standard across all trims. Side airbags and air curtains on Premium and Luxury. TPMS tire pressure monitoring on all models.

Comfort Seating

Leather seats on Luxury trim with 6-way electric driver adjustment, memory, welcome function, and 4-way lumbar support. Front seat heating and ventilation. Rear seat angle adjustable on Luxury. Microfiber steering wheel on Honor and Premium.

Intelligent Driving

ACC adaptive cruise, AEB, BSD, LDW, FCW, LKA, HMA on Luxury

Panoramic Sunroof

Available on Premium and Luxury trims with roof rack
