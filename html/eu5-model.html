EU5

Strong Power Performance, Intelligence Towards The Future
Test Drive

Pure Electric Performance

401km NEDC range with CATL ternary lithium battery (48.3kWh). 120kW maximum power and 240N·m torque deliver strong acceleration under 10 seconds 0-100km/h. Fast charge 30%-80% in just 30 minutes.

Intelligent Cockpit

9-inch central control screen with mobile phone interconnection. 7-inch LCD + LED instrument panel. PU steering wheel with multi-function controls. Manual anti-glare rearview mirror and dual sun visors with vanity mirrors.

Advanced EV Technology

Liquid-cooled CATL battery with 164.41 Wh/kg energy density. Single-speed EV gearbox with rotary shift and sports driving mode. Braking energy recovery system maximizes efficiency. Power consumption ≤13.7 kWh/100km.

Comprehensive Safety

ESP+TCS, ABS+EBD, EBA+BA, HHC, HDC, Auto Hold, TPMS standard

Spacious Interior

430L cargo capacity. Rear seat integral recline. 3 rear headrests adjustable.

Convenient Charging

Fast charging 30%-80% SOC in ≤30 minutes at ordinary temperature. Slow charging 0-100% in ≤9 hours. Low speed pedestrian warning and collision sensing automatic unlocking for enhanced safety.

LED Daytime Running Lights

LED DRL, penetrating taillight, emergency braking double-flash

Six Body Colors

Silver, Red, White, Blue, Gray, Black
