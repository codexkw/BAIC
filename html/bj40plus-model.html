Well Balance Between City And Wildness

BJ40 PLUS

All Terrain Adoption, High Load-Carrying, Part-Time 4WD System
Test Drive

Commanding Off-Road Presence

The BJ40 PLUS combines rugged off-road capability with urban sophistication. Its bold design language with black removable hard ceiling and front/rear red tow hooks make a powerful statement.

Purposeful Interior

12.3-inch LCD instrument cluster paired with a 10-inch infotainment display. The co-pilot illuminated BJ40 logo handle and anti-roll cage reinforce the vehicle's off-road DNA. 220V power outlet in trunk keeps you connected on every adventure.

Electronic Time-Sharing 4WD

Front and rear axle electronically controlled differential locks with lightweight front axle. Snow/Sport mode and Comfort/Economy mode for every driving scenario. 37° approach angle and 31° departure angle conquer the toughest terrain.

AT Tires & 17" Alloy Wheels

265/65 R17 all-terrain tires with blackened rims and red calipers

Leather Seats

6-way power driver seat with auto lumbar adjust. Rear 4/6 foldable for maximum cargo flexibility.

Dual-Screen Digital Cockpit

12.3-inch LCD instrument cluster with 10-inch central infotainment display. Easyconnection mobile connectivity, Bluetooth, AM/FM radio, and external video playback with 6-speaker audio system.

Comprehensive Safety

ABS+EBD, ESC, EBA, TCA, HAC, HDC, RMI electronic safety suite. Driver and passenger airbags with front three-point seat belts with pretensioner. Engine immobilizer and theft-deterrent system for complete peace of mind.

Ambient Atmosphere

Double cup holder ribbon lamp, interior point and ribbon atmosphere lamps

Separate Frame Construction

Double boom/helical spring front independent suspension with five-bar rear
