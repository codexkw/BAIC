Cheer For Your Life

U5 PLUS

Excellent Coefficient of Drag Performance, Library-class Quiet Cabin
Test Drive

Sleek Sedan Design

The U5 PLUS features an excellent coefficient of drag for outstanding aerodynamics. Penetrating LED taillights with flow turn signals and LED daytime running lights on Honor trim create a striking visual presence.

Library-Class Quiet Cabin

12.3-inch center screen paired with 7-inch and 12.3-inch LCD meters. Leather steering wheel with 2-way adjustment and multi-function controls. Manual anti-glare rearview mirror, dual sun visors with vanity mirrors, and front center armrest.

Dual Powertrain Options

1.5L engine with 83kW power and 142N·m torque. Choose between 5MT manual or CVT automatic transmission. Front-wheel drive with MacPherson front and H torsion beam rear suspension. EPB electronic parking brake with Auto Hold standard.

Smart Safety

ESP+TCS, ABS+EBD, EBA, HHC, TPMS standard. 360° panoramic image on Honor trim.

Comfortable Space

430L luggage volume with rear seat whole foldable. CN95 filter with pollen filtering A/C.

Connected Intelligence

BSD blind spot monitoring, LDW lane departure warning, and MOD low-speed dynamic object recognition on Honor. Bluetooth, radio, USB charging, and speed-sensing sound auto adjustment across all trims.

Available in Four Colors

Silver, Red, White, and Black body colors. Power sunroof standard on Honor, optional on Comfort. 16-inch aluminum alloy wheels with 205/55R16 tires.
