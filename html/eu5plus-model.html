EU5 PLUS

Where Strength Meets Intelligence On The Road
Test Drive

Urban Electric Elegance

R500 with 416km NEDC range or R600 with 501km range. 120kW permanent magnet synchronous motor with 240N·m torque. Fast charge 30%-80% in ≤30 minutes. Maximum speed 150km/h.

Generous Dimensions

4670×1820×1520mm with 2670mm wheelbase. 12.3-inch LCD instrument and 12.3-inch center screen. Leather steering wheel with multi-function controls. Automatic anti-glare rearview mirror on Honor/R600.

Full LED Lighting

LED front and rear lights standard across all trims. Automatic headlights with follow-me-home function. LED daytime running lights, emergency stop signal, and ambient lighting on Honor/R600.

Premium Interior

Micro-fiber sports seats, 6-way electric driver seat, wireless charging on Honor

Rigid Body Structure

ESP+TCS, ABS+EBD, HHC, HDC, Auto Hold, ISOFIX, DVR standard

Advanced ADAS

Honor and R600 feature AEB with vehicle and pedestrian detection, ACC adaptive cruise, FCW/PCW collision warnings, BSD blind spot detection, LDW lane departure, and AVM surround view. R600 adds V2L/V2V capability.

Dual Charging Options

Fast charge ≤0.5h (30-80%), general charge ≤8.5h (R500) / ≤10h (R600)

Intelligent Battery Management

Ternary lithium battery with intelligent temperature control system
