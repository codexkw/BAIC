Commanding Presence

The BJ80's imposing silhouette combines military-grade toughness with premium luxury, featuring a bold front grille and muscular body lines that command attention on any terrain.

Luxurious Interior

Nappa leather seats, matte mahogany accents, chamois leather headliner — the BJ80 Mount Everest edition wraps you in premium comfort befitting a flagship. Heated and ventilated front seats with driver memory function ensure every journey begins in perfect comfort.

10.25" LCD Meter + 8" Display

Dual-screen digital cockpit with 10.25 inch LCD instrument cluster and 8 inch central display, providing essential driving information and entertainment in an intuitive layout.

Premium Seating

Independent rear seats with heating and co-pilot boss key for rear passenger comfort

Precision Engineering

Every detail crafted with military-grade precision and attention to quality

Separate Frame Construction

Built on a rugged separate frame with double wishbone torsion bar spring independent front suspension and five connecting rod rear suspension. Part-time 4WD with mechanical rear differential lock conquers the most demanding terrains.

All-Terrain Mastery

39° approach angle, 33° departure angle, and 25° ramp angle. Multiple driving modes including Snowfield, Sport, Comfort, and ECO ensure optimal performance across every environment. The mechanical rear differential lock provides unstoppable traction.

Flagship Details

Chrome accents, power sunroof, and front boneless wiper with rain sensor

3.0T V6 Power

206kW max power and 420N·m torque paired with 8-speed automatic transmission

Military-Grade Safety

Comprehensive safety suite including ABS+EBD, ESP, TCS, HHC, HDC, Roll Movement Intervention, front and rear parking sensors, reversing camera, driving recorder, and 6 airbags for complete occupant protection.
