Leading The Fashion Trend

X35

Safe, Enjoyable, High Quality Driving Experience
Test Drive

Compact Urban SUV

The X35 leads the fashion trend with its dynamic design. LED headlights and daytime running lights standard across all trims. Roof rack and 16"/17" alloy wheels complete the sporty look.

Smart Interior

8-inch touchscreen with mobile connect and Bluetooth. Multi-function steering wheel with audio and cruise controls. USB charging, pointer instrument panel, and front central armrest for daily convenience.

Three Powertrain Options

Choose from 1.5T+6MT (110kW, 210N·m), 1.5T+CVT for effortless driving, or efficient 1.5L+5MT (85kW, 148N·m). MacPherson front and H torsion beam rear suspension. EPB electronic parking brake standard.

Safety First

ESP, ABS+EBD, EBA, TCS, HHC, TPMS, dual airbags + side airbags standard

16"/17" Alloy Wheels

205/60 R16 standard, 215/50 R17 double color on Premium trims

Versatile Space

390L luggage volume with rear seat 4/6 foldable. Panoramic sunroof on Premium trims, standard sunroof on Honor. Air conditioning with micro filter and rear seat outlets across all configurations.

Available in Four Colors

White, Brown, Red, and Blue body colors. Engine cover and bottom fender available on higher trims for a more refined look.
