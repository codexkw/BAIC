BEIJING AUTOMOTIVE GROUP CO., LTD.

referred to as "BAIC Group"

BAIC Group is a pillar enterprise in China's automotive industry. Founded in 1958, it has evolved into a large-scale enterprise group with a comprehensive industrial chain covering vehicle and component R&D and manufacturing, automotive service and trade, integrated mobility services, finance and investment, generating annual revenue exceeding RMB 480 billion.

CORE BUSINESS

BAIC off-road series sold 180,000 units throughout the year,  
representing a 22% year-over-year increase.

ARCFOX delivered 81,017 units, up 169.91% from the previous year.

BAIC Foton achieved total sales of 614,000 units with a market share of 15.8%.

Beijing Benz reached a cumulative production milestone of 5,000,000 units.
