JOY: KEYWORDS OF THE DESIGN CONCEPT ARE...

BAIC brand JOY concept car interprets the design concept of CAPITAL BEAUTY.
It will bring users a quiet sense of satisfaction and practical comfort.
The design of "confident, warm and Oriental charm" is the beautiful vision of the
BAIC brand for future travel.

We extracted the basic elements from the humanistic characteristics and urban
architecture of the East, and continued to study, aiming at the linear curvature design
and the processing of chamfer, the basic pattern was formed, with order and
temperature.

Regarding the design of the front face, we start from the basic graphics and combine the balanced and
stable number "five" to form a highly identifiable "five-hole element" design, representing the integrity of
the Eastern spirit. Combined with the requirements of different models of the BAIC brand, the five
elements of differentiation are formed to strengthen the visual identity of the BAIC brand.

The "poised" ratio. The JOY concept car is 5013 mm long, 2042 mm wide and has a wheelbase of 3000 mm. Large shaft ratio and low wind resistance profile bring stable posture and spacious interior space to the user.

"Leisurely" surface. The simple and pure curved design, combined with the strong wheel arch surface, creates a safe body image, with speed, and cutting sense of the line, reflecting the young fashion, as well as a sense of controlled movement.
