Intelligence

Embark on a journey into the future with our new car's intelligent driving functions. Enjoy the freedom that comes with intelligent driving, where every decision and maneuver is enhanced by cutting-edge technology.

Integrated Adaptive Cruiser Control (IACC)

It intelligently adapts to the preset speed and automatically switches targets, taking the load off your feet and allowing for a more relaxed driving experience.

Lane Departure Warning (LDW)

It aims to help drivers minimize the risk of unintended lane departures on highways, expressways, and main roads by both visual and auditory alerts.

Blind Spot Detection (BSD)

While driving, the blind spot detection system uses millimeter-wave radar to detect blind spots at the rear of the vehicle on both sides, reducing the risk of traffic accidents caused by blind spots.

Forward Collision Warning (FCW)

lt alerts the driver through auditory and visual warnings when the distance to the preceding vehicle becomes dangerously close, helping the driver maintain a safe distance from the vehicle ahead and prevent rear-end collisions.

Autonomous Emergency Braking (AEB)

It detects pedestrians or vehicles in front of the car and alarms if there is a risk of collision. Safety control, no more tailgating.

Hill Hold Control (HHC)

It prevents the vehicle from slipping, thus improving driving safety when starting on an uphill slope.

High Beam Assist (HMA)

Automatically turning on or off the high and low beams to avoid dazzling other drivers, promoting safer driving conditions.

Hill Descent Control (HDC)

It allows for a gradual descent to promote safety and comfort. The speed is automatically controlled to prevent accidents.

Powertrain

The golden powertrain of ALL NEW X7 is the result of a groundbreaking collaboration between BAIC and Meta, combining cutting-edge technology with expert engineering to create the perfect balance between power and precision.

2021"China Heart"Top Ten Engine Award "Magic Core" 1.5T Engine

Boasting a remarkable 39.2% thermal efficiency, 21 noise reduction technologies, and a 350bar+ high flow ratio.

High-flow VGT Turbocharger

With a 43% improvement in low-speed dynamic response time, lightweight design and efficient energy configuration bring exceptional performance to more than just supercars, providing a thrilling driving experience for all.
